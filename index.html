Submission Guidelines

Full paper preparation, templates, submission channel & presentation requirements for CASICAM 2026.

Guidelines for Paper Submission

Prospective authors are invited to submit full and original research papers which are not submitted, published, or under consideration elsewhere.

All submissions must be written in English and limited to 6 pages including figures (up to 2 extra pages allowed with over-length fee).

All papers must conform to the official CASICAM 2026 template. Use the MS Word template available via the link below.

CASICAM Template (Word)

The full papers should be submitted in PDF format via the EDAS platform link.

You must create (or use) an EDAS account and follow the provided instructions. Authors will be informed of acceptance per the published schedule.

If you require an invitation letter, please contact us at contact@casicam.com.

Guidelines for Presentations

  • All accepted papers require an accompanying presentation to appear in the final Programme.
  • Presentation duration: 15 minutes (strict).
  • Use the official PowerPoint template: CASICAM Presentation Template.
  • Prefer simple fonts (Arial, Calibri) with size 30+.
  • Avoid embedded videos to ensure compatibility.
  • Naming convention: PaperID.pdf, PaperID.pptx.

Publication Opportunities

All accepted research papers undergo rigorous peer review and will be published in a Scopus-indexed conference proceeding.

  • Springer Series
  • Archives of Materials Science and Engineering
  • Journal of Achievements in Materials and Manufacturing Engineering

Two best papers will be considered for publication in a Q1 Scopus-indexed journal.

Invitation to CASICAM'26

Dear Colleagues,

We are pleased to invite you to the 4th edition of CASABLANCA INTERNATIONAL CONFERENCE ON ADDITIVE MANUFACTURING (CASICAM), to be held in Marrakech, Morocco, on April 23–25, 2026.

CASICAM'26 is a scientific event organized by the Ecole Supérieure de Technologie de Casablanca (ESTC) and the Ecole Nationale Supérieure d'Electricité et de Mécanique (ENSEM) in collaboration with The Moroccan Association of Additive Manufacturing and 3D Printing (AMFAIM'3D).

It is an opportunity for national and international researchers, scientists, representatives of R&D organizations, engineers, industrialists, entrepreneurs, managers, doctors and architects to interact and share their research activities, ideas, developments and emerging applications of Additive Manufacturing and related technologies.

The conference will take place face-to-face and will feature renowned national and international speakers. It also offers a curated space for forging new connections in additive manufacturing and 3D printing, with dedicated networking moments to meet high‑level stakeholders.

We look forward to seeing you at CASICAM'26 in Marrakech, Morocco.

The official language of CASICAM'26 is English . Simultaneous interpretation is not provided; presenters should be able to communicate fluently in English.
